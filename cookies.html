Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, track usage statistics, and deliver relevant advertisements. Some cookies are set by us directly; others are set by third-party services we use.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function. They cannot be disabled. They include local browser storage used to remember your unit system preferences (e.g., kN-m vs. kip-ft, MPa vs. psi).

2.2 Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements that help fund this free service. Google may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Show personalized ads based on your browsing history and interests
  • Measure ad performance and click-through rates
  • Prevent the same ad from being shown repeatedly

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time via Google Ad Settings or Your Ad Choices.

2.3 Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site — for example, which calculators are most used and how long sessions last. This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the user experience.

3. Cookie Summary

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration

IDE, DSID Google AdSense Personalized advertising Up to 1 year

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers let you view, block, or delete cookies. See your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Google Ad Settings: adssettings.google.com — opt out of personalized ads.
  • Google Analytics Opt-Out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • NAI Opt-Out: optout.networkadvertising.org

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website (e.g., your unit preferences will not be remembered between sessions).
